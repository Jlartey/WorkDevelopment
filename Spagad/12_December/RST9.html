Item 5:

Equipment:

(A) Brick, Spoon, Doll, Knife, Pencil
(B) Box(open at top), Plate Cup

Procedure:

Put items(A) near the child and items (B) 6' further away

Lead-in-item:

   
Put the doll in the box     Not scored
(Leave it there)
Now, put the spoon in the box     Not scored
(Replace in original positions)
Now, put the doll and spoon in the box     Not scored

GIVE THE REQUEST AS A SINGLE UNIT. DO NOT BREAK INTO PARTS.

Test Request:

(a) Put the spoon and knife on the plate
(b) Give me the cup, the box and the doll
(c) Put the pencil in the box and the knife in the cup
(d) Put the brick under the box and give me the plate
